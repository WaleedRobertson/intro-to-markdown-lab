# Perfect Sandwich 


___15 minute prep time___ 

## Ingredients

* Bread

![bread](./Assets/pexels-polina-tankilevitch-5419309.jpg)

* Peanut Butter 

![Peanut butter](./Assets/pexels-karolina-grabowska-4202955.jpg)
* Jam 

![Jam](./Assets/pexels-cottonbro-7702451.jpg)
 

 ## Directions 

 1. Carefully apply peanut butter to one slice
 2. Apply jelly to the other silce 
 3. **Carefully** cut the sandwich in half (diagnolly at a 45 degree angle) 

 ## Level Up
 
 * Add bananas or chocolate syrup glaze
